Nova Frontiers - Interactive Attack Energy Combat V9

Files changed:
- backend/app/main.py
- frontend/src/main.jsx
- frontend/src/styles.css

Backend systems changed:
- Reworked real-time combat from automatic timed attacks into an interactive attack-energy combat session.
- Added attack energy max of 12.
- Added per-ship attack energy rate from 1 stack every 2-5 seconds based on ship combat rating, role, evasion, tier, and pilot combat/piloting/bounty skills.
- Normalized every combat weapon to a required attack energy cost from 1-5.
- Preserved the rule that every ship has at least one defensive weapon.
- Combat-oriented ships can field multiple weapons, up to 6 depending on available fitted weapons/profile.
- Added player combat actions:
  - weapon fire
  - defend
  - use all
  - utility module activation
  - escape using 2 attack energy
- Added backend endpoint action type: combat_action.
- Escape now requires 2 attack energy and still respects the 6-second failed escape retry cooldown.
- Defend now costs 1 attack energy and applies a short non-stacking +15% defense/dodge posture.
- Use All now spends available attack energy, selects the largest affordable weapons first, and queues follow-up shots so the salvo streams one action per combat beat instead of dumping every shot instantly.
- Enemy AI now has its own attack energy bar and spends energy on attacks/defense/utility when ready.
- Added non-stacking combat utility effects:
  - self attack buff
  - enemy defense debuff
  - enemy dodge debuff
  - hull repair
  - shield surge
  - evasion buff
  - target painter / accuracy debuff
  - armor hardener
  - capacitor injector
  - enemy attack debuff
- Utility strength scales by rarity/tier when generated from equipped modules.
- Active buffs/debuffs expire by timestamp and are included in battle payloads.
- Combat damage/hit calculations now account for active buffs, debuffs, defense posture, dodge, attack modifiers, and accuracy modifiers.

Frontend systems changed:
- Battle modal now shows a true attack energy system instead of passive replay only.
- Added player and enemy attack energy bars with 12 pips.
- Added weapon buttons under Your Ship.
- Each weapon button displays required attack energy inside the button.
- Added Defend button with required energy and effect hint.
- Added Use All button.
- Added utility module buttons with required energy and effect category.
- Escape button remains in the header and now shows Req 2 E under the text.
- Buttons visually disable when:
  - not enough attack energy
  - action recovery is active
  - battle is complete
  - escape cooldown is active
- Active combat effects are shown as chips under the battle stage.
- Enemy AI panel shows enemy energy and recovery state.
- Battle modal styling updated for the new interactive action deck.
