# CHANGELOG — Galaxy-Scale Faction War / Gates / Star Map

Backend:
- Expanded the universe to 12 galaxies and 54 planets/stations/gates.
- Added 3 galaxy-control factions:
  - Solar Accord
  - Iron Meridian
  - Umbral Veil
- Added faction tables and player faction assignment.
- Added galaxy metadata:
  - map coordinates
  - controlling faction
  - home/safe depth
  - capturable flag
  - center value
  - control bonus percent
  - war cooldown
- Home galaxies and one-hop shield galaxies are not capturable.
- Added galaxy gates and adjacency routes.
- Galaxy travel now requires being docked at a Jump Gate and can only move one adjacent galaxy at a time.
- System/planet view now includes gate nodes that jump to adjacent galaxies.
- Added galaxy war tables:
  - galaxy_wars
  - galaxy_war_presence
- Added galaxy war declaration:
  - must be adjacent to owned faction territory
  - opens after 24 hours
  - capture station is at galaxy center
  - active wars show a red king-of-the-hill capture ring
  - one faction alone in the ring starts a 5-minute countdown
  - capture resets if contested or empty
  - winner captures the galaxy and applies a 1-day cooldown
- Added weak-faction capture assistance:
  - faction behind by 1+ galaxy gets a small capture bonus
  - faction behind by 3+ galaxies gets a larger capture bonus
  - bonus is capture-only, not general economy/stat power
- Added center galaxy control bonuses:
  - strongest in the center
  - weaker toward faction home galaxies
- Added center-gradient content:
  - higher-tier ore is more likely near center galaxies
  - higher-tier ancient scan sites are more likely near center galaxies
  - higher-tier pirate bases are more likely near center galaxies
- Increased map activity/object density:
  - galaxy NPC traffic: 36
  - system NPC traffic: 42
  - galaxy ore sites: 18
  - system ore sites: 16
  - galaxy exploration sites: 16
  - system exploration sites: 14
  - pirate station global soft cap: 24
- NPCs are more aggressive in galaxies not owned by the player faction.
- Pirates/raiders/outlaws are even more aggressive in unowned galaxies.

Frontend:
- Added unified Star Map page with:
  - Galaxy View
  - Planet View
- Removed separate visible nav entries for Galaxies/System Map while keeping old routes for compatibility.
- Galaxy View shows faction control, capturable/home-safe status, center value, and control bonus.
- Planet View shows gate nodes to adjacent galaxies.
- Added Galaxy War page:
  - current faction summary
  - faction balance
  - ongoing/upcoming wars
  - war timers
  - capture hold timer
  - navigate-to button
  - capturable galaxy list
  - resolved war history
- Galaxy map renders red capture rings and capture station markers for active wars.
